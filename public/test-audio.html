🎤 Audio Test

Test your microphone and audio playback

Click "Start Test" to check your microphone

How to Test:

  • Step 1: Click "Start Test" and allow microphone access
  • Step 2: Speak and watch the audio visualizer move
  • Step 3: Click "Hear Yourself" to test playback (you'll hear your voice with a slight delay)
  • Step 4: If both work, your setup is ready for SecureVoice calls!
